File Sharing!

Our Motivation:

Why have we chosen "File Sharing" as our topic?

Because we believe that information should be free and available for everyone at any given time
We try to provide an easy way to share your files!

What should our Website do?

First of all it should be simple and user-friendly but still have a certain amount of style.

Focus in the 1. term:

The first thing to implement will be static HTML-Site which will be made fancier by using css.
We will write content to explain the risks of file sharing
and present some big file-sharing-plattforms.

Focus in the 2. term:

When the whole graphical part is over we will start to set up a server
where everyony can down and upload his stuff and
we will (possibly) create an Login-system with e-mail verification.
